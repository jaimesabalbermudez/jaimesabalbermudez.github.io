About Me

AI enthusiast and physicist. My interests mainly revolve around computer vision, reinforcement learning, and evolutionary algorithms; albeit narrow down to using mathematics and introspection to solve complex problems! I also have a deep interest in doing research in machine learning through introspection; I believe there is still a lot we can learn about how we learn (excuse the redundancy)! Evolution is inherently an efficient process, and even though our ability to perform any task is limited by our biology, we can learn from its concepts to create better AI (without the biological limitations we face), and possibly even solve the problem of Artificial General Intelligence in this manner!
